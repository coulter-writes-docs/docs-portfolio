# Get started with WorkHop

New to WorkHop? Use this quickstart guide to:

* [Part 1: Accept your invitation](#part-1-accept-your-invitation)
* [Part 2: Review your profile details](#part-2-review-your-profile-details)
* [Part 3: Respond to a survey](#part-3-respond-to-a-survey)


## Before you start

Before running this quickstart, ensure:

* You have received a WorkHop invitation email from your HR team.
* You have access to the email account the invitation was sent to.


---


## Part 1: Accept your invitation

First, you must accept your WorkHop invitation, create a password, and log in to your account.

### Open your invitation email

1. Open the WorkHop invitation email in your inbox.

>[!NOTE]
>If you cannot find the email, check your spam or junk folder. If you still cannot find it, contact your HR administrator.

2. Select **Accept invitation**.

    WorkHop opens in your browser and displays the account setup page.

### Create a password

1. On the account setup page, enter a password in the **Password** field.

    Your password must meet the following requirements:
    * At least 8 characters long
    * Contains at least one uppercase letter
    * Contains at least one number

2. Re-enter your password in the **Confirm password** field.
3. Click **Create account**.

    WorkHop logs you in automatically.


---


## Part 2: Review your profile details

Now, review and confirm your profile details.

1. In the top-right corner, click your avatar and select **Edit profile** from the drop-down menu.
2. Review your pre-filled name, preferred name, job title, and team values.
3. Adjust any information as needed, then click **Save** to confirm.

    WorkHop saves your profile and returns you to your **Dashboard**.


---


## Part 3: Respond to a survey

Now to fill out your first WorkHop survey.
> [!IMPORTANT]
> WorkHop surveys are anonymous. Your individual answers are never visible to your managers or HR team.

### Open a survey

1. On your **Dashboard**, find the **Surveys** section.

    Open surveys are listed here with their due dates. If you do not see any surveys, your HR team has not sent one yet.

2. Select a survey to open it.


### Fill out and submit the survey

1. Read the survey introduction, then click **Start**.
2. Answer each question on the page.

>[!NOTE]
>Questions marked with an asterisk (*) are required. You cannot submit the survey until all required questions are answered.

3. Click **Next** to move to the next page. Repeat until you reach the final page.
4. On the final page, review your answers. If you want to change an answer, click **Back** to return to the relevant page.
5. Click **Submit** to send your responses.

    Check your inbox and **Dashboard** regularly for more surveys.
